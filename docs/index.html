ESP Web Flasher

Flash firmware to your ESP32 board directly from your browser using WebSerial.

Browser Support: Please use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge on a desktop computer.
CRITICAL SAFETY WARNINGS:
  • Flashing 16MB firmware to a 4MB board will cause boot failure. Ensure your hardware matches the selected firmware.
  • ESP32 (Classic) firmware must NOT be flashed to ESP32-C3 and vice versa. These are different chip architectures.

Video Player + Audio (CYD – 4MB Flash)

Standard firmware for the default Cheap Yellow Display (CYD) board. Based on ESP32 (classic).

Video Player + Audio (CYD – 16MB Flash – MODDED BOARD ONLY)

Firmware for CYD boards modded with a 16MB flash chip. Do not flash to standard boards. Based on ESP32 (classic).

Remote Firmware (ESP32-C3)

Firmware for the Remote control board. Based on ESP32-C3 chip.
